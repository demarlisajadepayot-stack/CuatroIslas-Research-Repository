# Cuatro Islas Protected Landscape and Seascape (CIPLS)
Research Library

## About

This repository serves as the official open-access research repository for studies conducted within the Cuatro Islas Protected Landscape and Seascape (CIPLS).

The repository aims to:

- Promote research transparency
- Provide reference materials for future studies
- Support evidence-based management
- Facilitate knowledge sharing

## Research Categories

- Biodiversity and Ecosystem
- Environmental Quality and Resource Management
- Climate Change and Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR)
- Socio-economic
- Policy and Governance
- Emerging concern/s

## Citation

Researchers utilizing materials from this repository should properly cite the original authors.
